Why Advanced Checking Matters

Traditional password strength meters often focus solely on length and character variety. Our advanced algorithm considers additional factors such as:

  • Resistance to dictionary attacks
  • Presence of personal information
  • Use of uncommon character combinations
  • Resistance to brute force attacks based on current computing power
  • AI guessability factor

Tips for a Stronger Password

  • Use a combination of uppercase and lowercase letters, numbers, and symbols
  • Avoid using personal information or common words
  • Make it long - aim for at least 12 characters
  • Consider using a passphrase instead of a single word
  • Use a unique password for each account
  • Incorporate randomness to make it harder for AI to guess

Remember, the security of your online accounts depends on the strength of your passwords. Use this tool to ensure you're creating passwords that can withstand modern hacking techniques and AI-powered attacks.
